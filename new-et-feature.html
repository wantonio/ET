Product FAQ

  • Q:  Can Engineer’s Toolset monitor NetFlow?

    A:  Yes, the NetFlow Real-time tool captures and analyzes Cisco® NetFlow 5 & 9, JFlow, AppFlow, and sflow data in real time to show you what kind of traffic is traveling through the network, where it's coming from, and where it's going. With the NetFlow Real-time tool, you can display inbound and outbound traffic separately for granular analysis and troubleshooting. You can even view the last 5 to 60 minutes of flow data broken out by applications, conversation, domains, endpoints, and protocols. That way you know exactly how your bandwidth is being used and by whom.
